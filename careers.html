Culture

"We hire people who care deeply about craft — and give them the space to do their best work."

NORYX is not a place for feature-factory thinking. We're building something that requires deep focus, strong opinions, and a willingness to rethink assumptions.

We work asynchronously by default. No mandatory meetings. No status theater. Communication is written, decisions are documented, and output matters more than hours.

Every person on the team ships real work. There are no layers between you and the product. If that sounds like your kind of environment — keep reading.

Why NORYX

Remote-first

Work from anywhere. We're distributed by design, not by accident. Async communication and documented decisions keep everyone aligned.

Deep work time
